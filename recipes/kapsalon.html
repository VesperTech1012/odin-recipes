Kapsalon

This delicious dish contains the following ingredients:

  • Doner
  • fries
  • lettuce
  • garlic sauce
  • unions
  • spicy sauce
  • CHEEESSSEEE!!!

To make this dish just follow these steps:

  1. fill a dish with fries
  2. put all not sauce ingredients on it
  3. put it in the oven for like 10 minutes
  4. put sauces on
  5. eat!!
